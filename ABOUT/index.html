About us

Welcome to FRESKO – your trusted partner in Islamic mental health and personal growth

"Find guidance, strength, and peace through faith with Fresko."

At FRESKO, we are deeply committed to supporting individuals, especially Muslim youth, who face challenges such as LGBTQ tendencies, pornography addiction, and mental health struggles like depression. Guided by the timeless principles of Islam, we provide a safe, non-judgmental, and compassionate space for mentorship, healing, and self-recovery.

what do we do

At FRESKO, our mission is to serve as a guiding light for individuals, particularly Muslim youth, who are grappling with the challenges of modern life that can lead to spiritual and emotional struggles. We are dedicated to providing holistic mental health support, firmly rooted in the compassionate teachings of Islam.

We aim to empower individuals to overcome difficulties such as LGBTQ tendencies, pornography addiction, and depression by fostering a deeper connection to their faith and offering practical, faith-based solutions. Our mission is to create a nurturing environment where individuals feel seen, heard, and supported without judgment, enabling them to regain control over their lives and thrive in accordance with Islamic principles.

What Do We want to achieve

At FRESKO, we envision a world where Muslim individuals, especially youth, have the guidance, tools, and community they need to navigate the complexities of modern life while maintaining their faith and spiritual integrity.

We aspire to be a global leader in Islamic mental health support, bridging the gap between traditional faith-based values and contemporary psychological practices. Our vision is to create a society where mental health challenges such as LGBTQ tendencies, pornography addiction, and depression are addressed with compassion, understanding, and solutions rooted in the Quran and Sunnah.

what do we want to accomplish

At FRESKO, our goals are rooted in a deep commitment to addressing the unique challenges faced by Muslim youth in today’s world. We aim to create a comprehensive platform that not only provides solutions for mental health struggles but also fosters a community of support, guidance, and spiritual growth.

Our primary goal is to offer holistic care that addresses the emotional, psychological, and spiritual needs of individuals. This involves providing mentorship and personalized guidance tailored to help individuals navigate struggles such as LGBTQ tendencies, pornography addiction, and depression, all while reconnecting them with their Islamic faith. By integrating the timeless principles of Islam with modern psychological approaches, we aim to empower individuals to overcome these challenges and lead balanced, purposeful lives.
